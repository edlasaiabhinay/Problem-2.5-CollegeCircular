## 📌 Project Overview

Important college announcements and circulars often get lost in cluttered emails or WhatsApp groups. This platform provides a **centralized, targeted, and trackable announcement system** that ensures important information reaches the right audience effectively.

The system enables administrators to create, manage, and monitor circulars while allowing students and staff to receive personalized notifications and track important updates.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Build a targeted announcement platform where:

* Admins can create and distribute circulars.
* Notices can be targeted by department, year, role, or specific groups.
* Engagement and read status can be tracked.
* Urgent notices are prioritized and followed up automatically.
* Users receive notifications instantly through multiple channels.
